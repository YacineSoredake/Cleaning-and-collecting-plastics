Learn About Leaf Village

At Leaf Village, we believe in the power of collective action to combat plastic waste and protect our planet. Every year, millions of tons of plastic end up in our oceans, harming marine life and polluting our environment. By coming together, we can make a difference through small yet meaningful actions.

Our platform empowers individuals to take part in environmental conservation by:

  • Marking locations where plastic waste is found to make cleanup efforts easier.
  • Collaborating with like-minded individuals to collect and recycle plastic responsibly.
  • Encouraging sustainable habits within communities to reduce plastic usage.

Together, we can build a cleaner, greener future for generations to come. Join Leaf Village today and become a part of the solution!
